Pathing Help

-To make a bot path use F5, this will begin tracing. Start walking/running around map, your path will be recorded. Switch to seccondary weapon to pause bot on your location and switch back to primary to continue trace.
-To finish bot path use F6, to end tracing

-Use F7 to clear previous path.

-Using F8 will clear all traces

-To save the pathing you have done click F9
